Coast Empire Community Based
Organization
( C.E C.B.O )

is a C.B.O located in a Lakeview ward Naivasha, Kenya.
We deal with more of humanitarian services and support.

ABOUT US

General Information
The C. B. O was established in April 2019 and came to be registered officially in July 2019. It is located in a slum in Naivasha, Kenya.
Objective
To come in the help of the people with immediate help in terms of psycho social support, feeding the very vulnerable families and assist during the epidemics and pandemics.
What we do.
We deal with humanitarian services and support. Main areas of focuses being, basic help and empowerment for very vulnerable, health issues(HIV AIDS), child social protection, Gender based violence ( Gender Recovery Support).

Coast Empire C. B. O. Craving for the society.

OUR PROGRAMS

  • Helping the vulnerable people, the poor,
  • Gender Violence Recovery Support.
  • Soap and Jik making for the small business for the poor
  • Mat making programs for Empowerment.
  • Feeding the children at Monicah Memorial.

PROJECTS

Health Talks

Targets Youths and Young Mothers on ways to keep safe from contracting STDs and HIV & AIDS. We use participatory approaches in sharing this information. More so, we also provide sanitary towels and contraceptives.

Gender Violence Recovery Support

We run this program in Conjunction with Nairobi Women’s hospital Naivasha branch. The project was realized due to increased cases of assaults and murder attempts. Most of the victims are women and children. We identify and forward these cases to the hospital for treatment and medications. We also do follow ups and offer guidance and counselling. GVRS will remain to be part of the organization’s program to make sure that the community continue living in harmony.

Mat and soap Making Empowerment program

This programs commenced early September last year to bridge the gap between unemployment rates and self-employment during the Covid pandemic. The program targets both Youth and women in the community. It is a sustainable program because beneficiaries are still reaping its fruits till today. This project is a member contribution as well as well-wishers. Due to low resources, more than 300 people have benefitted as a result of this program.

Solomon Mugilwa Ambetsa

My name is , I am a resident in Kihoto, Lakeview, Naivasha, Kenya. I am happy and proud of the Coast Empire CBO. The CBO once assessed me in my house at Kwa Mukurino plots, where I was very desperate, I was lacking food, unpaid rent and no jobs due to pandemic. It was on May 10th 2020, whereby Kihoto had experienced flood as a result of Lake Naivasha overflow due to heavy rains. The Coast Empire Coordinators, Sir Anthony and Mathew, helped me a lot. First they gave me food for one week, talked with my landlady and later mobilized funds from friends of Coast Empire and paid for me arrears of ksh 5000/. They later engaged me to soap selling, they make soap as coast empire and sell by themselves. Sometimes they give me at very low cost as 20/ per half a litre and I sell at 30/. The profit I made helped me to maintain myself and upto today I sell the soap. The soap is locally made with the CBO and it is waterbased soap. I am most grateful again for Coast Empire and continue helping the vulnerables and uplifting them. Where I am today I can confidently say is because of this Youthful CBO.
