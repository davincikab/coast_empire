Coast Empire Community Based Organization
Location
Located in a slum in Naivasha, Kenya.
Objective
To come in the help of the people with immediate help in terms of psycho social support, feeding the very vulnerable families and assist during the epidemics and pandemics.
What we do.
We deal with more of humanitarian services and support. The C. B. O was established in April 2019 and came to be registered officially in July 2019. Coast Empire members are Community social workers from within their hearts.
